## Changes made by Chess Scan

Chess Scan extracted the twelve piece variants from the exported Figma sheet, normalized them for reusable inline SVG rendering, replaced the original navy and pale-blue palette with Chess Scan's forest, paper, gold, red, and blue tokens, and adjusted optical sizing for small board squares. The board annotations and tactical glyphs are original Chess Scan work, redesigned with the source set's bold, rounded, two-tone visual language.
